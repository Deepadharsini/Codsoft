Projects

Smart Plant Monitoring System using IoT

A Smart Plant Monitoring System using IoT is an innovative solution that leverages sensors and internet connectivity to monitor and maintain optimal conditions for plant growth. The system uses sensors to track key environmental factors such as soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and light intensity. These sensors collect real-time data, which is transmitted to an IoT platform for analysis.

Based on the collected data, the system can provide notifications or take automated actions, such as watering the plants or adjusting lighting. The system can be accessed remotely via a smartphone or web application, allowing users to monitor and control plant care from anywhere. This technology is highly beneficial for precision agriculture, indoor gardening, and smart home applications, promoting healthier plant growth with minimal manual intervention.

Freshfleet - Online Grocery Shopping Site

This project aims to develop a centralized online grocery platform utilizing the MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js) stack. The platform will provide a user-friendly interface for customers to browse and purchase groceries, while offering vendors a platform to manage their products and deliveries. Key features include vendor and customer account management, real-time delivery tracking, secure payment processing, product reviews, chatbot support, personalized recommendations, direct farm purchases, and detailed nutrition information. By integrating these functionalities, the platform seeks to streamline the grocery shopping experience, enhance customer satisfaction, and support local farmers.
